VEDIC COSMOLOGY VIDEOS

Our Vedic Cosmology Videos section has many videos related to the subject from a number of devotees within the International Society for Krishna Consciousness (ISKCON). The subject is extremely complex and sometimes confusing because we are dealing with a subject that has different levels of reality, gross and subtle, often even inconceivable. As a result, sometimes even the opinions of educated researchers may differ on specific topics, what to speak of differing from the perspectives of gross material scientists who only deal with direct sense perception. Keep an open mind as you watch these videos and take in as much as you can at one time. Think about it and let us know if you have any questions. Contact us at tovp2016@gmail.com with your inquiries.

  Disclaimer: The ideas on Vedic cosmology expressed in the videos and audios on this page may not all represent the views of the TOVP Cosmology Department.

Navigate through different video categories by clicking on tabs above the gallery. You can also scroll through different videos by using arrows above and below the gallery, which appear automatically when there's more than 8 videos per page.
